# Product Definition

This document describes the product vision, goals and constraints for your project. Defining the product clearly helps the planner and reviewer agents make informed decisions and keeps the development effort aligned with user value.

## Value Proposition

Explain the core problem your product solves and why it matters. Identify the primary user personas and the benefits they will gain.

## Goals and Non-Goals

List the goals that this project aims to achieve. Be explicit about what is out of scope to avoid scope creep.

## User Personas

Describe each type of user who will interact with the product. Include their needs, motivations and pain points.

## Success Metrics

Define how success will be measured. Common metrics include user adoption, performance improvements or revenue targets.

## Constraints and Assumptions

Document any technical, regulatory or organisational constraints. List assumptions that have been made about the environment, dependencies or users.

## Stakeholders

Identify key stakeholders and their roles in the project. Clarify who is responsible for decisions, funding and approval.
